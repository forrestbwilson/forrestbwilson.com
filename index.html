Teamesteem

Teamesteem is a non-profit 501(c)(3) based in Washington, D.C. with the mission to cultivate conscious, aware, globally minded leaders able to execute with and through people for a higher purpose. We provide high school and college students with access to mentorship and experiential learning opportunities with our local partners. For our annual summit, we invite leaders in business, government, and non-profits to share wisdom and business experience. We also have a job shadow program, book clubs, ideas nights, and a mentorship network where we pair students with mentors and partners in our network with opportunities and experiences they don't have access to in the traditional educational model. We currently serve over 100 students in the DC area. I Co-founded Teamesteem and served as the President and Executive Director for 2 years
