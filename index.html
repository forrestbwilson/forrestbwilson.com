Dormie

For that reason, I invented a mobile app (available for iOS and Android) to connect competitive golfers. For this project, I put together and led a team of 4 through the design (UI) and user experience (UX) process, app development, and co-creating a proprietary algorithm to rank golfers on our in-app leaderboard

- The UX/ UI process included creating user personas, user stories, a user-centered business canvas, mockups, and leading user feedback sessions

- App development included serving as scrum master, putting together sprint schedules, and working with engineers to develop our software. Tech stack: React Native, Redux, Apollo, React Navigation, Expo, LoDash, GraphQL, GraphCool, Lottie, Flexbox

- Co-creating proprietary algorithm included working with our data scientist on prototyping and designing algorithm to fit our golf use cases. We wrote the algorithmn in Python.

- Marketing included social media strategy and management, website design, commercial video production, attending golf trade shows, and hosting events to generate awareness for our product

Teamesteem

Co-founder, Chairman of The Board

Teamesteem

Teamesteem is a non-profit 501(c)(3) based in Washington, D.C. with the mission to cultivate conscious, aware, globally minded leaders able to execute with and through people for a higher purpose. We provide high school and college students with access to mentorship and experiential learning opportunities with our local partners. For our annual summit, we invite leaders in business, government, and non-profits to share wisdom and business experience. We also have a job shadow program, book clubs, ideas nights, and a mentorship network where we pair students with mentors and partners in our network with opportunities and experiences they don't have access to in the traditional educational model. We currently serve over 100 students in the DC area. I Co-founded Teamesteem and served as the President and Executive Director for 2 years

ITNL International Private Limited

After graduating from college, I moved to Singapore to start my career working for a Mumbai based infrastructure financing and development firm, ITNL.

My job responsibilities began with researching our target countries for projects we would be interested in and qualified for based on geo-political climate, risk allocation, and sources of funding. As the project moved forward, I was responsible for managing the qualification and proposal process both internally and externally.
