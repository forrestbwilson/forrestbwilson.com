Dormie

Inventor and Founder

Dormie

I've played competitive golf my entire life and I've always found it difficult to find other golfers to play with at my skill level. For that reason, I invented a mobile app (available for iOS and Android) to connect competitive golfers. For this project, I put together and led a team of 4 through the design (UI) and user experience (UX) process, app development, and co-creating a proprietary algorithm to rank golfers on our in-app leaderboard, business development, and marketing the app to golfers.

- The UX/ UI process included creating user personas, user stories, a user-centered business canvas, mockups, and leading user feedback sessions

- App development included serving as scrum master, putting together sprint schedules, and working with engineers to develop our software

- Co-creating proprietary algorithm included working with our data scientist on prototyping and designing algorithm to fit our golf use cases

- Business development included networking and partnering with golf courses, leading industry professionals (Top 100 golf instructors), and golf brands

- Marketing included social media strategy and management, website design, commercial video production, attending golf trade shows, and hosting events to generate awareness for our product

Additionally I led the team through personal, team, and organizational development exercises, including feedback sessions, training on adult-development polarities, and introduction to various system dynamics models. I also worked with and coached team members individually on their own capacity for leadership, how to show up fully and authentically, and how to bring their best self to the team, app, and project.

Teamesteem

Teamesteem is a non-profit 501(c)(3) based in Washington, D.C. with the mission to cultivate conscious, aware, globally minded leaders able to execute with and through people for a higher purpose. We provide high school and college students with access to mentorship and experiential learning opportunities with our local partners. For our annual summit, we invite leaders in business, government, and non-profits to share wisdom and business experience. We also have a job shadow program, book clubs, ideas nights, and a mentorship network where we pair students with mentors and partners in our network with opportunities and experiences they don't have access to in the traditional educational model. We currently serve over 100 students in the DC area. I Co-founded Teamesteem and served as the President and Executive Director for 2 years
